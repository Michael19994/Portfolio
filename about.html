About

In early 2022, I decided I wanted a new challenge, that challenge was to change career. I had been exposed to coding in my previous work which gave me a little insight into it.

This stirred my curiosity to trying out YouTube videos and after doing some research where I could take my learning to the next level I discovered CodeClan by a friend’s recommendation.

I picked the professional software development course which lasted four months. This was a fantastic experience for me as I was able to learn in a welcoming and comfortable environment where I got to meet many great people who shared a similar goal to me. The instructor’s at CodeClan were patient and understanding which added to the experience.

I get real enjoyment out creating anaesthetically pleasing websites that are also accessible to people from all walks of like and has fantastic functionality so that users can have the best experience possible whilst using the website.

For myself I really like being able to bring out that creative side of me where I can produce a product to that of a extremely high standard.

I also enjoy the learning process that comes with software development as I find it very rewarding. I wil continue to improve and acquire further knowledge to help me self improve in my trade.
